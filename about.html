ABOUT US

Mission and Vision

Vision

To be recognized as the leading organization of experts, committed to promoting Maritime Health and Safety in the West Pacific region.

Mission

We advance Maritime Health through continuing education of members and promoting research that provides value in policy formulation on issues of seafarer fitness determination, health promotion, and safety at sea.

We seek to develop connectivity with stakeholders in Maritime Health affairs and to build meaningful cooperation with DOG-HFSRB, MARINA, ILO-IMO, and similar agencies, inside and outside our country.
